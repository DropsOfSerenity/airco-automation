Energy Solutions by Design.

Since 1992

Since 1992 Airco Automation, Inc. has been providing products and services intended to maximize comfort, security, and efficiency for all types of building applications - Commercial, Educational Institutions, Government Entities, Healthcare Facilities, Hospitality Industry, Industrial Facilities, and Technology Facilities to name a few.

Airco Automation, Inc. uses products that enable building owners and property managers to integrate, automate, and manage their buildings easily and efficiently by combining them onto one network and one computer graphical user interface using automation software. Building Automation Systems (BAS) greatly increase occupant comfort, lower energy use, and allow off-site building control from anywhere in the world.

We strive to maximize value by selecting the solution and system that represent best resolution to our valued clients.
